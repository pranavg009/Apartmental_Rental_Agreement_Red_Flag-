RENTAL AGREEMENT (SAMPLE 2 - SYNTHETIC, FOR TESTING ONLY)

This agreement is made between the Landlord and the Tenant for the residential unit located at
45 Lake View Apartments, Pune.

1. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a refundable security deposit equal to five (5) months' rent at the time
of signing this agreement.

2. NOTICE PERIOD
The Tenant must give 60 days' written notice before vacating. The Landlord may terminate
the agreement by giving 15 days' notice.

3. LOCK-IN PERIOD
There shall be a lock-in period of 11 months. The Tenant must pay the full remaining rent
for the lock-in period if they vacate early, regardless of reason.

4. RENT INCREASE
The Landlord reserves the right to increase the rent at their discretion during the lock-in
period with 15 days' notice.

5. PAINTING CHARGES
An amount equal to one month's rent shall be deducted from the deposit towards painting
charges upon vacating, regardless of the condition of the walls.

6. MAINTENANCE
The Tenant shall bear all maintenance costs including structural repairs during the tenancy.

7. TERMINATION
The Landlord may terminate this agreement at any time without cause by giving 15 days' notice.
The Tenant may not terminate before completion of the lock-in period under any circumstances.

8. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall pay a fixed utility charge of Rs. 3000/month regardless of actual usage.
