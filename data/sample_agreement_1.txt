RENTAL AGREEMENT (SAMPLE 1 - SYNTHETIC, FOR TESTING ONLY)

This agreement is made between the Landlord and the Tenant for the residential unit located at
123 Green Park Road, Bengaluru.

1. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a refundable security deposit equal to two (2) months' rent at the time of
signing this agreement. The deposit shall be refunded within 30 days of vacating the premises,
subject to deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

2. NOTICE PERIOD
Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 30 days' written notice to the other party.

3. LOCK-IN PERIOD
There shall be a lock-in period of 6 months, during which neither party may terminate the
agreement without mutual consent, except in case of a documented emergency.

4. RENT INCREASE
The monthly rent shall be increased by 5% annually upon renewal, with prior written notice
of at least 60 days.

5. PAINTING CHARGES
The Landlord shall be responsible for painting the premises before the Tenant moves in.
No painting charges shall be deducted from the deposit unless the Tenant causes damage
beyond normal wear and tear.

6. MAINTENANCE
The Landlord shall bear the cost of structural repairs. The Tenant shall bear the cost of
minor day-to-day maintenance such as light bulb replacement.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement as per the notice period clause above. No penalty
shall be charged for termination after the lock-in period ends.

8. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall pay for electricity and water as per actual usage, billed monthly.
