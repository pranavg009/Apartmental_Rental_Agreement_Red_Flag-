RENTAL AGREEMENT (SAMPLE 3 - SYNTHETIC, FOR TESTING ONLY)

This agreement is made between the Landlord and the Tenant for the residential unit located at
7 Sunrise Towers, Gurgaon.

1. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a security deposit equal to ten (10) months' rent. The deposit is
non-refundable if the Tenant vacates before 24 months.

2. NOTICE PERIOD
The Tenant must give 90 days' written notice. The Landlord may terminate the agreement
immediately at any time without notice for any reason.

3. LOCK-IN PERIOD
There shall be a lock-in period of 24 months. Early termination by the Tenant for any reason
shall result in forfeiture of the entire deposit plus a penalty equal to three months' rent.

4. RENT INCREASE
The Landlord may increase the rent by any amount at any time without prior notice.

5. PAINTING CHARGES
Painting charges equal to two months' rent shall be deducted from the deposit unconditionally,
whether or not the Tenant repaints the unit themselves before vacating.

6. MAINTENANCE
The Tenant shall bear full responsibility for all repairs including structural damage,
regardless of cause.

7. TERMINATION
The Landlord may enter the premises at any time without notice and may terminate the
agreement and repossess the property without court process if rent is delayed by even
one day.

8. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall pay all utility charges as determined solely by the Landlord, with no
itemized bills provided.
